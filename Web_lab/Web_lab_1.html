Halong Bay
Date - 02.04.21
Written by Нгуен Чан Хоанг Чунг
Tags - #Bay, #Vietnam, #Place_to_visit

The karst seascape of Halong Bay is one of the world's most spellbinding sea views
and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Thousands of limestone islands sit
within this bay in the Gulf of Tonkin, eroded into jagged pinnacles
by wind and water action over millennia.
